Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

DPC Developments, LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Big Brain mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we may collect your name and email address through our authentication provider.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics such as feature usage frequency and app performance data to improve the App.

AI Conversations: When you use the AI Tutor feature, your questions are sent to our AI service provider (OpenAI) for processing. We do not store, read, or log the content of your AI conversations on our servers. Your conversations are processed in real-time and are not retained by us.

Locally Stored Data: Study notes, flashcards, quiz results, and preferences are stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This data is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly use a sync feature.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's features and functionality
  • To process your AI Tutor questions through our AI service provider
  • To manage your subscription and account
  • To improve the App based on anonymous usage patterns
  • To send you important updates about the App (with your consent)

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: Processes AI Tutor queries. OpenAI's API data usage policy states that API data is not used to train their models and is retained for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring purposes. See OpenAI's API Data Usage Policy.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: Handles subscription payments and account management.
  • DuckDuckGo: Provides web search results for the AI Tutor's search feature. No personal data is shared with DuckDuckGo.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. API keys and sensitive credentials are stored securely and never exposed in the App's code.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is designed for students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

6. Data Retention

Account data is retained as long as your account is active. Locally stored data (notes, flashcards, quizzes) remains on your device until you delete the App or clear its data. AI conversation data is not retained by us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Export your locally stored data

8. Subscription Information

Big Brain Pro subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store or Google Play Store. Payment is charged to your Apple ID or Google Play account at confirmation of purchase. Subscriptions automatically renew unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage and cancel your subscription in your device's account settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last updated" date.
